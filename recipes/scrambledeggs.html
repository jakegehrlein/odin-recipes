Scrambled Eggs Recipe

They're scrambled eggs. You've had them before. It's amazing you need a recipe for this.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional, for creamier eggs)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, chopped herbs, diced vegetables, cooked bacon or sausage

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add the milk (if using), salt, and pepper. Whisk until the yolks and whites are fully combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat. Add the butter or oil and let it melt, coating the bottom of the pan.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Let it sit undisturbed for a few seconds until it starts to set around the edges.
  4. Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs, pushing them from the edges toward the center. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat. The residual heat will finish cooking the eggs. If desired, sprinkle with cheese or other toppings and let it melt.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with additional herbs or toppings if desired.
